WCTA Cybersecurity Club

CyberPatriot (The National Youth Cyber Defense Competition)

CyberPatriot is a national cybersecurity competition where student teams act as IT administrators tasked with securing virtual machines. Each round challenges us to identify vulnerabilities, harden systems, and maintain critical services under time pressure.



National Cyber League (NCL)

The National Cyber League is a collegiate and high school cybersecurity competition where individuals and teams solve real-world challenges in cryptography, open-source intelligence (OSINT), network traffic analysis, log analysis, password cracking, and more.
